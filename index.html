Membership Card

Where to buy

The Marketplace Card is available at any Algonquin Campus, and is free to aquire. Like using your Algonquin Campus card, it can be loaded with money to make purchases, and tracked online.

Marketplace Points

As you make payments through your card, or with any other form of payment, you will begin to collect points. For every dollar spent, you will aquire 15 points (1 point=1 cent). When enough points have been collected you can purchase meals or drinks with your membership.

Information

Location

The Marketplace is located in Algonquin College’s Woodroffe Campus, in the D building. You do not need to be a student to access the restaurants, or sign up for the membership card.

Health

Here at the Marketplace, we take food quality seriously. We know the importance of maintaing a healthy diet, and make sure we use only the best ingredients from local sources.
